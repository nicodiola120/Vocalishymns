Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Vocalis ("we", "our", or "app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Vocalis does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information you provide. The app operates entirely offline — all hymn data and downloaded audio files are stored locally on your device.

Advertising

Vocalis uses Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect and process certain device information and usage data to deliver relevant ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Approximate location (if enabled on your device)
  • App interactions and usage data
  • Device type and operating system version

This data is collected and handled by Google in accordance with the Google Privacy Policy. You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps at https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

In-App Purchases

Vocalis offers a "Remove Ads" in-app purchase. All purchase transactions are processed by Google Play and are subject to Google's privacy policy. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Data Storage

Hymn audio files you download are stored locally on your device. You can delete them at any time through the app or your device settings. No audio data is transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

Vocalis integrates the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — for serving advertisements (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play In-App Billing — for processing purchases (Terms of Service)

Your Choices

You can opt out of personalized advertising by resetting your Android Advertising ID in your device settings (Settings > Google > Ads > Reset advertising ID). You can also purchase the "Remove Ads" option within the app to disable all advertisements.

Children's Privacy

Vocalis is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: vocalis.app@email.com
